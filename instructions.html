how to play

Creating/Joining Games

  1. The player who wants to send the first message will name and create a game.
  2. Other players can join that game up until the game creator starts the game.
  3. Players will play in the order that they join the game.

Gameplay

  1. The first player will decide on a message, type it out, and send it.
  2. When every other player receives the message, a "show message" button will appear. Once clicked, the message will be displayed for a few seconds.
  3. Then, a textbox will appear and you will need to write what you saw and pass on the message.
  4. At the end, the first and final messages will be compared to determine if your team succeeded or failed!
